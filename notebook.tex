York that lends money to small businesses. The project I'm working on
involves modeling using the past applicants data to predict the outcome
of future applicants to decide whether to approve or reject the loan.
The problem faced during this modeling is lack of data -\/- since it's
been only 4 years since the firm started and we don't have enough data
to work with. However, we decided to use bootstrap sampling and simple
hypothesis (logistic regression, decision trees) to build a classifier.
Although we had more than 25 variables, I will name some of the most
important ones.

The top features are 1. FICO score 2. Length of credit history 3. Annual
Revenue 4. Time in business 5. Utilization of credit (\%)
